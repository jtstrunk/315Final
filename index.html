Our History

Omicron Media Unlimited was founded in 2005 by John Smith, a seasoned event planner with years of experience in the industry. Initially, the company focused on organizing small-scale events such as corporate meetings and product launches, with a focus on delivering high-quality service and attention to detail.

Over the years, the company expanded its services and began organizing larger and more complex events such as conferences, festivals, and concerts. In 2010, the company hired a marketing team and started investing in online and offline advertising to promote their events, resulting in increased attendance and revenue.

In 2015, the company went through a major restructuring, with John Smith stepping down as CEO and handing over the reins to his daughter, Sarah Smith. Under Sarah's leadership, the company continued to expand and innovate, leveraging new technologies such as virtual and hybrid events to reach a wider audience.

Today, Omicron Media Unlimited is one of the leading media event companies in the region, with a reputation for delivering exceptional events that exceed expectations. The company employs over 50 staff members and has organized hundreds of successful events for clients across various industries, from tech startups to Fortune 500 companies.
